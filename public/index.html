About

Hi, I'm Leon and I'm a Game Developer. I currently study Games Engineering and I'm working on a few projects on my own, which you can check out down below. What made me get into developing games was the joy of games themselves and the joy of solving problems. There's nothing more satisfying than making an elegant solution to a complex issue work. When I'm not coding I'm probably playing guitar (mostly metal), reading or cooking something delicious. So if you want to jam, have a good book or recipe to share or just want to make some games, hit me up!

Projects
Skeletoons
Skeletoons is a lowpoly stealth and puzzle game based on Unity and C#. The player controls a skeleton who's scared of the other scary creatures on the graveyard and needs to escape. It features challenging puzzles, clever level design and beautiful graphics through Unity's light and post processing tools.
Wallther - Dont let the wall falter
In this arcade game the player needs to keep the wall guarding the city intact while the enemy army attacks. The challenge lies in managing the resources efficiently and finding the perfect set of upgrades to stay on top of the barrage of enemy projectiles.
