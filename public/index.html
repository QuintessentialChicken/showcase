Projects
Skeletoons
Skeletoons is a lowpoly stealth and puzzle game based on Unity and C#. The player controls a skeleton who's scared of the other scary creatures on the graveyard and needs to escape. It features challenging puzzles, clever level design and beautiful graphics through Unity's light and post processing tools.
Wallther - Dont let the wall falter
In this arcade game the player needs to keep the wall guarding the city intact while the enemy army attacks. The challenge lies in managing the resources efficiently and finding the perfect set of upgrades to stay on top of the barrage of enemy projectiles.
Office Dogs
Office Dogs is a VR Puzzle/Escape Room Game built with Unreal Engine 5 and Blueprints. The player needs to manage the work assigned to them while spending enough time on finding the hidden keys to escape the office. The development posed a unique challenges due to the nature of VR, especially dealing with motion sickness during movement.
Game Engine - WIP
With this project I'm developing my own game engine from scratch to get a better understanding of the inner workings of games and their implementation. I'm using C++ and DirectX11 and I'm taking special care to follow the appropriate design patterns to be able to adapt, extend and refactor my code at any point.

You can check my progress on Github and on my dev log
The Tale of a Senile Wizard
This puzzle game was developed during the Summer SemesterGameJam 2020 and features a senile wizard who occasionally remembers his spells. The theme was "Modification" which we implemented through spell books that allow the wizard to modify his environment with powerful spells. One freezes water, one slows down time and one even changes the perspective
Space Disaster
This game was our entry for the Winter SemesterGameJam 2020 with the theme "Malfunction". The malfunction in our game is the space ship the player is stuck on. Everything seems to fall apart and it's only a matter of time until the ship completely fails...

Unless you can fix everything in time.
CHIP8 Emulator
After being introduced into the world of emulators by Dolphin (Nintendo Wii) and Yuzu (Nintendo Switch), I was fascinated by the way these programs can act like an entire console and run games in their place. I wanted to get a better grasp on what's going on, so I started where everyone starts: Writing a CHIP8 emulator
FreeZwap
FreeZwap was the result of my first ever game jam. The theme was "Freeze" which we made into the core mechanic of our game: Freezing your position in space. The momentum is stored, so unfreezing during a jump will send you flying on the original arc. Paired with ten increasingly challenging puzzles, we made it to 4th place.
